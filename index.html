Lessons

Lesson 1: Basic Piano Keys

In this lesson, you will learn the names of the piano keys and how to locate them on the keyboard. Understanding the layout of the keys is essential for learning how to play.

Lesson 2: Reading Sheet Music

Sheet music is the written form of music. In this lesson, you will learn how to read basic musical notation, including the staff, clefs, and notes.

Lesson 3: Playing Simple Scales

Scales are fundamental in music. This lesson will teach you how to play simple major scales, which are essential for developing finger strength and technique.

Lesson 4: Chords and Harmony

Chords are groups of notes played together. In this lesson, you will learn basic chords and how to use them to create harmony in your music.
